Pink Lemonade

Lemonade but Better.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

1 min.

serving

5 servings

Tags : Kids Sour Pink drinks

instructions

step 1

Place the watermelon, sugar, lemon juice, and 1 cup of water in a blender. Blend until Smooth.

step 2

Place a fine mesh sieve over a pitcher and pour the mixture through the sieve.

step 3

Add the remaining water to the pitcher and stir until combined.

step 4

Chill and serve over ice then enjoy!

ingredients

1/2 cup of Lemon Juice

1/2 cup of Granulated sugar

1/2 cup of Freshly squeezed lemon Juice

4 cups of cold water

tools

Blender

sieve
