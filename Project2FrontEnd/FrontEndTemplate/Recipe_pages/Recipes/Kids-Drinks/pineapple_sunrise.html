Pineapple Sunrise

Flavourful sunrise drink.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

1 min.

serving

1 servings

Tags : banana smoothie healthy drinks

instructions

step 1

Stir together the pineapple juice and 7UP. Pour over ice.

step 2

Carefully pour grenadine into drink so it settles on the bottom.

step 3

Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

step 4

Serve and Enjoy!

ingredients

4 Oz of Pineapple Juice

2 Oz 7UP

1 tb spoon Grenadine

maraschino cherries for garnish
