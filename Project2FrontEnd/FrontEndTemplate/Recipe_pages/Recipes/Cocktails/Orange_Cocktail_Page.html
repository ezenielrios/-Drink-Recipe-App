Orange Cocktail

A refreshing cocktail made with fresh-squeezed orange juice!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : orange cocktail alcohol drinks

instructions

step 1

Add ice, vodka, orange liqueur, and orange juice to glass, stir well.

step 2

Top off with soda. Garnish with orange slice and mint. Enjoy!

ingredients

2 oz. vodka

3 oz. fresh squeezed orange juice

1 oz. orange liqueur

4 oz. lemon-lime soda

mint, orange slices for garnish
