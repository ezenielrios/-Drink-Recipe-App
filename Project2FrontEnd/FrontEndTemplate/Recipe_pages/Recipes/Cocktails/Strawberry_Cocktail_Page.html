Strawberry Cocktail

A simple and refreshing cocktail made with strawberries.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : strawberry cocktail alcohol drinks

instructions

step 1

Gather ingredients, and muddle the strawberries in a glass.

step 2

Add ice and vodka, top off with lemonade.

step 3

Garnish with strawberry and lemon slices. Serve and enjoy!

ingredients

4 strawberries, sliced

1 1/2 oz. vodka

3 oz. lemonade

lemon slices for garnish

tools

Muddler
