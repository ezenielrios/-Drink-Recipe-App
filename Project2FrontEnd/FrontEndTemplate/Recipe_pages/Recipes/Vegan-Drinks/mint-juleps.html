Classic Mint Juleps

These traditional mint juleps are a staple in the South--especially for the Kentucky Derby. Whip them up at home with this easy recipe.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Vegetarian Vegan Nut-Free Soy-Free

instructions

step 1

Place mint and sugar in a medium pitcher. Using a muddler or the handle of a wooden spoon, muddle for about 60 seconds by pressing the mint against the bottom of pitcher to release its fragrance.

step 2

Do not tear or shred the mint. Add seltzer and bourbon; stir to dissolve the sugar. Divide ice among 6 rocks glasses and pour the bourbon mixture over the ice.

step 3

Garnish each glass with a sprig of mint.

ingredients

¾ cup fresh mint leaves, plus 6 sprigs for garnish

2 tablespoons superfine sugar

2 cups chilled plain seltzer water

¾ cup Kentucky bourbon

6 cups crushed ice

tools

Pitcher

Spoon
