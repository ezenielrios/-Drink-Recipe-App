Chocolate Milkshake

A thick and creamy treat that is easy to make.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : chocolate milkshake dessert drinks

instructions

step 1

Add ingredients to the blender, blend on high until smooth.

step 2

Top with whipped cream. Serve and enjoy!

ingredients

6 cups of chocolate ice cream

1/3 cup of milk

1/3 cup of chocolate sauce

whipped cream

tools

Blender
