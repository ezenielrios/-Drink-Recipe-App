Banana Smoothie

A great tasting source of potassium and protein.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : banana smoothie healthy drinks

instructions

step 1

Prepare the ingredients by slicing the banana into small pieces.

step 2

Add ingredients to the blender, blend on high until smooth.

step 3

Serve and enjoy!

ingredients

1 large banana (frozen)

1/2 cup milk

1/2 cup yogurt

tools

Blender
